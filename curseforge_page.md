<div align="center">
  <h2>🇺🇸 English (US)</h2>
  <hr style="width: 50%; margin: auto;">
</div>

<br>

<p align="center"><strong>Arcadia Admin Pannel</strong> is the ultimate administration solution crafted specifically for the <strong>Arcadia: Echoes of Power</strong> server.<br>Empower your moderation team with a robust GUI that streamlines player management.</p>

<h3 align="center">✨ Key Features</h3>

<p align="center">
👤 <strong>Player GUI</strong> — Manage online & offline players effortlessly.<br>
🎒 <strong>Smart Inventory (InvSee)</strong> — View, edit, and clear player inventories in real-time.<br>
🔄 <strong>Progress Control</strong> — One-click progress reset for streamlined testing/fixes.<br>
📍 <strong>Offline Tracking</strong> — Access homes & last locations via <em>FTB Essentials</em> integration.<br>
⚠️ <strong>Warning System</strong> — Dedicated JSON-based storage (<code>warns.json</code>) to track player discipline.<br>
🛠️ <strong>Admin Suite</strong> — Kick, Ban, Unban, and Teleport directly from the interface.
</p>
